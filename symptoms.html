Symptoms of Covid-19 1st Wave

COVID-19 affects different people in different ways. Most infected people will develop mild to moderate illness and recover without hospitalization.

  • fever
  • Tiredness
  • Dry Cough
  • Headache
  • Loss of Taste/Smell
  • Sore Throat
  • Aches and Pains
  • Shortness of Breath
  • Loss of Speech/Movement

Symptoms of Covid-19 2nd Wave

COVID-19 2nd Wave affects different people in different ways.

  • Extreme lethargy
  • Weakness
  • Pink Eyes
  • Conjunctivitis
  • Dry Mouth [Not Enough Salva]
  • Diarrhoea
  • Skin Rashes
  • Fatigue
