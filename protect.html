take steps to protect yourself

Wear A Face Mask

Make sure it covers both your nose, mouth and chin. When you take off a mask, store it in a clean plastic bag, and every day either wash it if itâ€™s a fabric mask, or dispose of a medical mask in a trash bin.

Wash Your Hands

Washing hands with soap and water is the best way to get rid of germs in most situations. If soap and water are not readily available, you can use an alcohol-based hand sanitizer that contains at least 60% alcohol.

Avoid Close Contact

Avoid close contact with people who are sick. If possible, maintain 6 feet between the person who is sick and other household members.
